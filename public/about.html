About ShadeSafe Vancouver

ShadeSafe Vancouver helps people find safer, cooler, and more comfortable outdoor areas by providing shade-related information for Vancouver.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make outdoor planning easier during sunny or hot days. ShadeSafe Vancouver helps users understand where shade may be available and make better decisions before going outside.

Who Is This For?

This app is designed for students, commuters, families, outdoor workers, and anyone who wants to plan safer outdoor activities in the city.

Data Note

ShadeSafe Vancouver is a student project. The information shown in this app is for planning support and should not replace official weather, health, or emergency alerts.
